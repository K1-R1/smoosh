# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment of any kind
- Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a
[private security advisory](https://github.com/K1-R1/smoosh/security/advisories/new)
on GitHub — this ensures your report is visible only to the project maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response
deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions
that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.
